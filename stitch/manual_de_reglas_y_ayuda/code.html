Inquiries & Clarifications

Can I change my prediction mid-roll? expand_more
Once the dice have been called to the pit, all predictions are finalized. Integrity is the hallmark of the Grand Salon.
What defines a 'Dirty Roll'? expand_more
A roll is considered dirty if a die fails to land flat or leaves the designated surface. In such events, a total reset of the turn occurs.
How are 'Escaleras' ranked? expand_more
High Escaleras (4-5-6) supersede Low Escaleras (1-2-3) during tie-breaker evaluations.
